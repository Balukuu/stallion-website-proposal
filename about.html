About Stallion Insurance Brokers

Home › About Us

Our Ugandan Heritage & East African Vision

Founded right here in Kampala, Uganda, Stallion Insurance Brokers Limited emerged from a distinct need: East African families and businesses required an insurance advocate who truly understood their local realities.

We saw that insurance was often overly complex and slow to settle claims. Since our inception, we have championed a client-first philosophy, shifting the balance of power from the massive underwriters directly back to you, the policyholder.

Today, as an officially licensed broker by the Insurance Regulatory Authority of Uganda (IRAU), we negotiate, secure, and manage thousands of policies annually, serving everyone from boda-boda fleet owners and commercial farmers to enterprise healthcare networks across the nation.

What Drives Us Forward

Our Mission

To provide bespoke, reliable, and prompt risk management advisory services that protect the financial ambitions of our clients across Uganda and East Africa.

Our Vision

To universally be recognized as Uganda's preferred provider of risk management, pioneering accessible and trustworthy insurance solutions for every sector.

Our Values

We anchor our daily operations in total Integrity, continuous Client-Centric Innovation, Transparency in claims, and an unwavering commitment to Professional Excellence.

Regulated by the IRAU

Stallion Insurance Brokers operates under the strict regulations of the Insurance Regulatory Authority of Uganda. What does this mean for you? It guarantees that your premiums are handled securely, your claims are legally protected, and our advisory services meet the highest national compliance and ethical standards.
