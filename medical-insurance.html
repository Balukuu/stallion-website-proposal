What is Medical Insurance?

Accessing quality private healthcare in Uganda can be extremely expensive out-of-pocket, particularly for chronic illnesses, maternity needs, or sudden surgical emergencies. While basic state healthcare or NSSF provisions exist, true financial protection and premium care require private medical cover.

A Stallion-managed Medical Insurance policy ensures that when illness strikes, your only priority is getting well—not worrying about how to pay hospital bills. We design customized health plans that grant you access to top-tier hospitals in Kampala and across East Africa.

What It Covers

Inpatient Care

Hospitalization, surgery fees, ICU charges, and specialist consultations.

Outpatient Services

Routine doctor visits, laboratory tests, prescription drugs, and diagnostics.

Maternity Care

Prenatal, delivery (normal and C-section), and postnatal care for mother and baby.

Dental Care

Routine checkups, extractions, fillings, and essential dental surgeries.

Optical Care

Eye tests, prescription frames, and lenses.

Evacuation

Emergency medical evacuation locally or regionally (e.g., to Nairobi) if required care is unavailable.
